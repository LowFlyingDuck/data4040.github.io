The Night makes the Day

A good sleep is worth a good week XD

Sleep is essential for your

  • Mental Health

    Without sleeping, your Brain doesn't get the rest it needs to recover from the stressful everyday life these days. In order to make sure not to be left with permanent psychological damage, you have to give it the sleep it needs.

  • Physical Health

    The same thing as in Mental Health applies to Physical Helth, too. If your Body doesn't receive the nightly rest, it will let you down at some point.

  • Brain Processing

    Your Brain doesn't only use the night for rest, it also needs it to process all the experiences and situations, that you faced throughout the day. By dreaming, your brain trains for real life problems.
